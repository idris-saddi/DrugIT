About us

DrugIT is an innovative project that aims to revolutionize the process of drug discovery and development through the integration of AI and biology. The pharmaceutical industry faces numerous challenges in identifying and developing new drugs, and the DrugIT project is designed to overcome these challenges by utilizing cutting-edge technology and scientific knowledge.


Try the Model

Our primary goal

is to develop a platform that streamlines the drug discovery process by leveraging AI and biology to predict the efficacy and toxicity of candidate molecules. By using molecular descriptors and deep learning algorithms, the platform is able to predict the activity of molecules against specific targets, such as enzymes or receptors. The predictions are then validated using experimental data obtained from in vitro and in vivo assays, providing accurate assessments of the therapeutic potential of the candidate molecules.

The DrugIT project

has the potential to significantly accelerate the drug discovery process by reducing the time and resources required to identify and develop new drugs. By leveraging the power of AI and biology, the platform is able to provide accurate predictions of drug activity and toxicity, enabling researchers to focus their efforts on the most promising candidate molecules. Additionally, the platform has the potential to reduce the cost of drug development by identifying potential issues with candidate molecules early in the process, before significant resources are invested.

Our team

the DrugIT project combines the expertise of biologists, Software Engineers and data scientists, who work collaboratively to develop and refine the algorithms and models that power the platform. The biologists are responsible for designing and conducting the experimental assays to validate the predictions made by the AI models. Meanwhile, the data scientists are responsible for developing the AI models that predict the efficacy and toxicity of candidate molecules.
